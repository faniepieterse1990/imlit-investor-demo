Business model

Multiple revenue paths without forcing a heavy first product.

iMLit can begin with a premium reader experience, then expand into enhanced editions, publisher partnerships and education licensing as the content pipeline matures.

Freemium reader access

A free reading tier introduces standard books, sample immersive scenes and local preferences.

Premium subscription

Subscribers unlock enhanced reading modes, richer soundscape packs, personalisation and cross-device continuity.

Enhanced immersive editions

Publishers can release premium editions with approved sound, atmosphere, haptic and gaze-aware layers.

In-app immersive packs

Genre-specific atmosphere packs support fantasy, thriller, study, learning and bedtime reading use cases.

Publisher revenue share

iMLit shares revenue on enhanced editions and provides a review workflow before any immersive layer goes live.

Education and institution licences

Schools and universities can license accessibility-friendly reading environments and classroom analytics.

Author analytics

Future opt-in analytics help authors understand chapter completion and engagement without biometric identity analysis.

Future marketplace

A curated marketplace can sell approved immersive layers, licensed content bundles and classroom experiences.

Investor logic

The web MVP is intentionally lightweight: it proves willingness to experience reading differently before building a costly native stack, content marketplace or publisher ingestion pipeline.
