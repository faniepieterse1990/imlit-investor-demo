Camera, gaze and demo privacy

Eye tracking is optional, local and clearly labelled.

This browser MVP uses camera access only after an explicit tap. It does not request microphone access, store footage or transmit camera data.

Camera is used only for browser-based gaze estimation.
Camera is optional, but required for eye tracking.
No footage is recorded.
No footage is uploaded.
No footage is stored.
No microphone access is requested.
Gaze data is processed locally for the demo.
Demo continues in manual mode without eye tracking if permission is denied.
Browser permissions can be revoked by the user.
Camera stream is stopped when disabled or when leaving the demo.
No biometric identity analysis is performed.

Honest capability label

When supported, the demo uses webcam-based eye tracking prototype technology with calibrated gaze-triggered reading. If gaze tracking fails, the UI states: “Eye tracking unavailable in this browser. Demo continuing in manual mode.”
